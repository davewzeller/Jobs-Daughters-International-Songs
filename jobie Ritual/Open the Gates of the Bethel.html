Open, Open, Open the Gates of the Bethel

Open the Gates of the Bethel


Open the gates of the Bethel with joy as we sing our sweet lay.

Open your hearts oh ye Daughters, that Love may enter today.

Hark to the sounds of rejoicing

As Love, Peace and Joy Ever Reign.


Voices with gladness and rapture

Are swelling, are swelling and carry the happy refrain.


Voices with gladness and rapture

Are swelling with Joy, with Joy again.


Open the gates of the Bethel for the fairest Daughters today.

Open your hearts to each other;

That Love dwells with us always.

Praying for meekness and Patience

With Faith in the Lord Divine.


Voices with gladness and rapture

Are swelling are swelling in tune with the heavenly chime.

Voices with gladness and rapture

Are swelling with Joy, with Joy sublime.
